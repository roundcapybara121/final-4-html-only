Why an online plant shop?

Nowadays, people go online to shop for everything -– from clothing to food. The main appeal of online shops is that they are convenient. Online shops allow businesses to remove a mediator between the seller and the buyer and streamline the checkout process.

Plants are often sold in small boutiques without an online presence. What if we were able to help these small businesses flourish by pushing their services online?

Understanding the design

Basic Interations

Here are components to consider for a shop:

Aggregator

  • Something to tally up the total cost of plants → allow customers to quickly know when they’ve reach their budget
  • Something that keeps track of what plants are in the cart → allow customers to quickly know what items they’ve added so far
  • A way to remove items from the cart → allow customers a way to recover from mistakes without having to restart the process

Search Operations

  • A way to sort the different plants → allows customers choose how to best search through the different selections
  • A way to filter between different properties of plants → allows customers to easily view only the items that they want

Vision Board

Take a look at what inspired my final design!

What I noticed with these shops was that they had varying hues of green and a complementary color to contrast the green. This makes the overall look very pleasing to the eye. Additionally, the edges, corners, and font have a refined look to them by using rounded corners and bouncy font.

Future Improvements

Here are things I would like to add if given more time for this project

  • Make the website more personalized by adding an “about” section into the header
  • In case the store sold more items, add a search bar to allow searching for items by name
  • Include tax and shipping costs in the aggregator
  • Add more ways to filter. For example, by color, high or low water needed, or specific heights

Conclusion

React is a powerful tool in that we can manage states. This allows for us to code interactions such as button presses. In the future, I hope to be able to utilize this tool further and create a more developed e-commerce platform.
